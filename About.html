About Me

I am a 4th year student from Reseda, California studying Computer Science at Azusa Pacific University.


My main interest in Compuer Science is Web Development. I enjoy Web Development for many reasons. One of those reasons is that I enjoy the satisfaction of building the website. As I built this personal website, I enjoy the feeling of the progress that I have made. I am very willing to put the extra time and effort to become more knowledgeable in this field. When I started my studies as a Computer Science major, I had barely any knowledge of Computer Science. The first language I learned was Java, which is the languaged that was used in most of my Computer Science classes. Since I developed an interest in Web Development, I have devoted most of my spare time researching and learing languages and applications that will benefit my employers and I in the future.


Volunteer work is something that I have been doing to get experince in different fields of Computer Science. I have been working with a professor at APU(Azusa Pacific University) to create games that are designed for children from elementary and below. The company the is called Creative Play, and it is still in the developing stages. Another volunteer opportunity that I was able to be involved in was a month long trip to Namibia. The trip was from May 8, 2016 - June 1, 2016. This was an action team composed of 10 Computer Science majors, including myself. The action team's purpose was to assist NUST (Namibia University of Science and Technology) students and professor in the creation of a health informatics system for public and private hospitals to use.
